Homeschool Martial Arts in Tyler, TX

Homeschool martial arts is coming soon to Bares Taekwondo Fitness: daytime Taekwondo classes built around a homeschool schedule, in Tyler, TX.

PE, Discipline, and Rank, During the School Day

Martial arts checks the boxes homeschool families are already trying to fill: physical education, structure, goal-setting, and social time with other kids, all inside a curriculum with real progression. Your student earns rank on the same standards as every student here, just on a schedule that fits your day.

Not Homeschooling? You're Still Welcome

Daytime classes are open to anyone whose schedule frees them up during the day: shift workers, flexible schedules, early risers. If daytime training fits your life, this class is for you.
